Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is one of the most influential technologies of the Fourth Industrial Revolution. It involves creating systems and machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as understanding natural language, recognizing patterns, making decisions, and learning from experience. AI has a profound impact on our daily lives. Personal assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant utilize AI to help manage our schedules, answer questions, and control smart home devices, enhancing our convenience and productivity. In healthcare, AI is revolutionizing the field by improving diagnostics, personalizing treatment plans, and predicting diseases through the analysis of vast datasets. Moreover, AI is transforming transportation with self-driving cars and smart traffic management systems, promising to reduce accidents and optimize traffic flow.
